About Dr. Peng W. Chee

Professor of Cotton Breeding & Genetics

Dr. Chee integrates wild relatives, quantitative-trait-locus (QTL) mapping and DNA marker technology to develop elite upland cotton lines with superior fiber length/strength and durable root-knot-nematode resistance.

Education

  • Ph.D. Plant Breeding & Genetics, Texas A&M University (1997)
  • M.S. Nanjing Agricultural University
  • B.S. Anhui Agricultural University

Affiliations

We're Looking For

We routinely recruit high-energy students and post-docs with skills in:

  • Quantitative genetics & mixed-model analysis
  • High-throughput DNA/RNA sequencing & bioinformatics
  • Cotton agronomy & large-plot field phenotyping

Funding comes from competitive grants (USDA-NIFA, NSF-PGRP) and Cotton Incorporated fellowships.

Prospective candidates should email Dr. Chee a single PDF comprising CV, unofficial transcripts and a 1-page statement of interests.
